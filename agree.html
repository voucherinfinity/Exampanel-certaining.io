Welcome to the Certaining Exam Tutorial

This tutorial is intended for candidates who will be taking one of the Beingcert Certification Exam at a Beingcert testing centre. The tutorial is optional and provides information to assist you in understanding what your exam panel will look like, how to navigate through it, and how to use tools that can help you to answer the questions.

  • This Exam Consists of 20 Question
  • This Passing Score is 60 or above
  • The Maximum Time Available For Completing This Exam is 30 Minutes


This is how the Exam Panel will look like. it consists of the following

  • 1. The examination uses Multiple Choice questions. Usually, this type of question has one correct answer To complete each Multiple-Choice question, click on the option that you believe to be the best response
  • 2. At the top right corner of the screen your username will be displayed
  • 3. The amount of time remaining is displayed at the top of the screen

You will receive a warning message when 5 minutes will be remaining for the exam

  • 4. Click the "Previous" button to move to the previous page or previous question
  • 5. Click the "Next" button displayed at the bottom of the screen to move to the next page or next question
  • 6. In addition to the navigation buttons, you can use the numbered buttons displayed in the question palette at the right side of the screen If the exam includes more than forty questions, you will need to scroll down arrow to navigate to additional questions
  • 7. The question numbers in the question palette change appearance to indicate different question states Active, Answered. Unanswered, Not Visited, and Marked for Review
  • 8. You can Review a question as a reminder to go back and check your answer or attempt it later To Review a question, select an option and click on the "Mark for Review" button displayed at the bottom of the screen. In the question palette the numbered question will be highlighted with the red colour Click on the "Mark for Review" button again to deselect the mark for review option
  • 9. Once the answer is chosen, the numbered question in the question palette will be highlighted in green colour To change your response, click on a different answer
  • 10. The unanswered questions will appear in black colour in the question palette
  • 11. The active question will be highlighted with yellow colour, whereas the unvisited questions will not be highlighted
  • 12. In Exam Status, you can click on Answered, "Not Answered, Not Visited, and Mark for Review' button. The list of subsequent question numbers will pop-up You can select any question number from the list and can jump back to that
  • 13. After attempting each question and reviewing your answers, you can end the exam by clicking the "End Exam" button in the bottom right corner of the screen Once the button is clicked, a pop-up window will appear asking you to confirm whether you want to end the exam Please note, selecting the End Exam" button during any portion of the exam, will end the exam session and submit any completed answers Any questions that are incomplete will be marked as incorrect

This concludes the tutorial. If you have time remaining, you can review the questions by clicking on the "Back" button to move back one screen at a time, or use the numbered buttons displayed on the right side of the screen
